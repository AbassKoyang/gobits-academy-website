ABOUT US

In today's increasingly digitized world, having digital skills is essential for success in almost any field. Unfortunately, many young people from less privileged backgrounds do not have access to the resources and training necessary to develop these skills. This is where GObits comes in - a program designed to equip young people with the digital skills they need to succeed in the modern world.
GObits is an innovative program that provides digital skills training to less privileged youths. The program's primary goal is to bridge the digital divide by providing access to technology and training for young people who might otherwise not have access to it. The program is specifically designed to help young people from low-income households, who may not have access to technology or the financial resources needed to pursue traditional education or training programs.
Through GObits, young people can learn a wide range of digital skills, including coding, web design, digital marketing, and data analysis. These skills are highly sought after in today's job market and can provide young people with a pathway to a successful career. By providing access to these skills, GObits is helping to break down barriers and provide opportunities for young people to succeed.
